SMS

Collect and monitor only financial transaction text messages including the name of the transaction party. The purpose is to conduct a credit risk assessment of the transaction and the description of the transaction amount. This credit risk assessment speeds up loan disbursement without any personal text messages being deleted, read or collected

Contacts

The NBFC loan process and guidelines mandates us to verify references. This feature enables us to do that & Why type in contact number when you can just choose from your contact book.

Camera and Photos

Allow us to capture images of your documents and images for loan application.

Location

User location helps to locate and verify the address which helps in making better Credit risk decisions and fasten the Know Your Customer (KYC) process.

Device

Allow us to collect and monitor your relevant device hardware information to establish your identity.

Phone State

Phone status is only used to record and view specific information. This information from users can help us better view any unauthorized actions on this device, as this will allow us to better detect fraud and potential risks.
