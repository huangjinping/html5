Permissions Disclosure

Introduction

In compliance with the applicable Data Protection Laws in Nigeria, including the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), SilkLoan is committed to safeguarding your personal information and maintaining transparency in data processing. To deliver a seamless loan application process and determine your credit eligibility, we require specific permissions to access certain data on your device. All collected information is securely encrypted, transmitted, and used solely for the purpose of providing our financial services. Your data will never be shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Permissions Overview

1. SMS Permissions

We seek access to your SMS messages to assess your loan eligibility and develop your credit profile. Upon your explicit consent, our app will access your SMS information for initial eligibility screening. Only filtered SMS messages will be collected and transmitted.

All SMS data transmitted to our server (https://middleware.silkloan.net) is securely encrypted.

2. Notifications

SilkLoan may send push notifications for loan updates, payment reminders, and promotional offers. These notifications are optional, and disabling them will not impact your app usage.

3. Emergency Contacts

To verify your identity and prevent fraud, we request access to two emergency contacts. You may manually enter these contacts or select them from your address book.

  • Data Usage: Only the selected contacts are accessed.
  • Security: Contact details are encrypted and stored securely.

4. Device Information

We collect device-related data for fraud prevention and risk management, including model name, OS version, serial number, and installed apps (financial category only).

5. Network Information

To ensure secure and reliable service, we collect Google Advertising ID (GAID), IP address, Wi-Fi network status, and SIM operator details.

6. Location

We request access to your rough location to detect potential fraudulent activities, such as offshore account access.

7. Application List

To assess risk and prevent fraud, we collect details about installed financial apps on your device.

8. KYC Requirements

To comply with Know Your Customer (KYC) regulations, we collect name, phone number, gender, date of birth, marital status, and government-issued ID.

9. Call Log

Call logs are no longer collected in the latest online version. However, older versions may still collect call logs for phone number verification.

Data Storage and Security

All data collected by SilkLoan is securely transmitted to our servers using advanced encryption protocols. If you do not submit a loan application, no data will be uploaded or stored.

How We Use Your Data

  • Verifying your identity.
  • Building credit scoring models.
  • Processing loan applications and determining eligibility.
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring account security.

Data Sharing and Transfer

We may share your data under the following circumstances:

  • Subsidiaries or Affiliates: For performance evaluation and service improvement.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: To facilitate credit-related services.
  • Legal Compliance: To fulfill legal or regulatory obligations.
  • Fraud Prevention: With risk management and information security providers.

By using SilkLoan, you agree to the data collection, usage, and sharing practices described above.
